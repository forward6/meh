The Multiverse Employee Handbook

“The Multiverse Employee Handbook,” curated and produced by Robb Corrigan, is a mind-bending journey through interdimensional corporate culture. Navigate quantum coffee breaks, file expense reports across eleven dimensions, and ponder the ethical dilemmas of dating your parallel-universe self. With reality-warping wordplay and cosmic satire, this podcast transforms mundane office life into a surreal adventure, leaving you questioning your job, existence, and the true purpose of quantum staplers.
