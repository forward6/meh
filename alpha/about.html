About The Show

Where quantum physics meets office humor, and corporate culture collides with cosmic uncertainty. Think "Office Space" meets "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy," but with more paperwork and fewer towels.

The Mission

The Multiverse Employee Handbook transforms complex scientific concepts into hilarious workplace analogies. Each week, we explore the bizarre intersection of quantum physics and office life, making both science education and corporate existence a little more entertaining.

The Idea

Created by Robb Corrigan, The Multiverse Employee Handbook emerged from a simple question: "What if understanding quantum physics was as straightforward as navigating office politics?" (Spoiler alert: neither is straightforward, but both are fascinating.)

The Format

Each 25 minute-ish episode blends:

  • Real quantum physics concepts
  • Original sci-fi narrative comedy
  • Workplace humor that transcends dimensions
  • Existential questions about office supplies

Perfect For

  • Science enthusiasts with a sense of humor
  • Office workers questioning reality
  • Anyone who's ever wondered if their printer problems might be caused by quantum uncertainty
  • Corporate employees seeking interdimensional career advancement
